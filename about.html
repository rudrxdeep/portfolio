About!

Why I love Practicing coding. Everyday

Rudradeep Gupta

Hey there! I'm a third-year Computer Science student at Bengal Institute of Technology, passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems and create meaningful impact.

Since childhood, I have studied in boarding schools, first in Kurseong, West Bengal (St. Anthony's School and Reliance Residential school) and then in Chattarpur, Delhi (CSKM Public School). These places taught me a lot how to survive in every situation possible, a never giving up attitude, and a will to solve any difficult problems thrown at me.

Ever since I was young, I've been fascinated by the power of technology to transform lives. This curiosity led me to pursue a degree in Computer Science, where I've gained hands-on experience in coding, web development, and data analysis. I thrive in environments that challenge me to think critically and creatively, whether it's developing a website or analyzing complex datasets.

What excites me most about technology is its potential to drive innovation and positive change.

One of my strengths as a student is my ability to adapt quickly to new technologies and learn continuously. I'm always seeking opportunities to expand my skills, whether through online courses, youtube or projects . My goal is to merge my technical skills with my passion for social impact to create solutions that address pressing societal challenges.

When I'm not coding or studying, you can find me exploring the outdoors, playing table tennis, or at the gym. These experiences outside the classroom have shaped my holistic approach to problem-solving and enriched my perspective as a student and future technologist.

I'm eager to connect with fellow students, mentors, and industry professionals who share my passion for technology and innovation. Let's collaborate, learn from each other, and make a difference through technology together!
